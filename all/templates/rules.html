Rules of the game:

You should know that you're the king (or queen) of here, but we have a few rules to keep the game fair:

  1. Be Respectful: Attack ideas, not people. Profanity, insults, and bullying are strictly prohibited.
  2. Follow the Law: Sharing illegal content is strictly forbidden. This is just a Open-Source project, don't get yourself (or us) in trouble.
  3. You Leave a Trace: You are free, but not invisible. For security purposes, your IP addresses are logged. In cases of violation, this data may be used for legal proceedings.
  4. Right to Remove: Content that violates the rules is not protected by "freedom of speech" and will be removed.
